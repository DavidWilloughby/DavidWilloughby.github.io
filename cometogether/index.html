What is ComeTogether?

CT works by connecting English learners with other learners and mentors in their local area. Learners use the CT app to expand their daily contact with English and prepare a weekly task that they share with others in their group.

How is it different?

CT promotes learner-directed study and there are no ‘lessons’. Instead, users are encouraged to meet up every week to practice what they’ve learned. There’s also the option of partnering with a mentor in order to get expert feedback on the weekly task.

What level of English do I need?

CT is for people who have a good knowledge of English grammar and vocabulary and who want to improve their spoken interaction. It’s not designed for beginners, and you'll need to be able to hold a basic conversation in English in order to participate.

How can I use it?

First, you’ll need to download the free CT app and register with one of our low-cost plans. Once registered, you’ll have access to the weekly task as well as the contact details of learners and mentors in your area.

Membership

Membership costs ¥4,800 per month and includes:

1. Access to the CT learning environment via smartphone app

Each week you’ll receive a new set of prompts to explore English and a weekly task with plenty of guidance to help you through it.

2. Access to our Learner Directory

Search for other learners in your area by age, gender and interests, and arrange to meet up to practice the weekly task. You can contact as many other users as you like.

3. Access to our Mentor Directory

If you’d like to practice with a fluent English speaker, you can search for a mentor in your local area. Most mentors charge a small fee for their time.
